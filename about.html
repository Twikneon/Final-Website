Board of Director.


Mr. Sushan, 61, is President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company and has been a director since 2014.
• From 2017 through 2019, Mr. Sushan served as President and Chief Executive Officer of ServiceNow, Inc., provider of enterprise cloud computing services for global enterprises
• From 2008 through 2015, Mr. Sushan served as President and Chief Executive Officer of eBay, Inc. (“eBay”), provider of the global eBay.com online marketplace and PayPal digital payments platform.
• Mr. Sushan joined eBay in 2005 as President of eBay Marketplaces, responsible for eBay’s global e-Commerce businesses.
Prior to joining eBay, Mr. Sushan was the Chief Executive Officer and Worldwide Managing Director of Bain & Company from 1999 to 2005, and a Managing Director from 1992 to 1999.
Mr. Sushan is Chairman of PayPal Holdings, Inc. In addition to this public company board service, he also serves on the Board of Trustees for The Bridgespan Group. Mr. Sushan served on the Board of Directors of Intel Corporation from March 2009 until May 2017, and ServiceNow from March 2017 until June 2020.
